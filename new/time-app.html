Project Background

This App originated from the HCI course on Coursera. This course was taught by a professor from UC San Diego. This course introduced some basic skills about HCI and the professor gave us three topics to choose as the assignment topic. I chose Time and I finished an APP related to time step by step. All my work followed the instruction of the course.

Motivation

How to be more efficient and make good use of time are discussed frequently. Time management is more and more important to everyone. And some people even get procrastination, which is that people always put off matters until the last second. They always regret in the end of day for they have do nothing. Certainly, there are some methods, like writing down on a notebook what you need to do, or record something in a software. In addition there are some software or APPs which can sync to different devices to remainder people in different situations in time. Although these APPs can remainder people to do things they have arranged before, people cannot clearly evaluate whether they are efficient, whether they have managed time well and whether they can save more time.

As many people said, time is money. Perhaps we can record the time just as record money , and after a week or a month, we may review on a graphic about how we spend our time in the past time and if we can save more time when doing something. There are some similar time and money management software existed, however, there are no one which focus on the time recording. In order to develop such a software or an APP (Maybe in the following weeks, I will design an APP first.) , I observed three individuals how they usually manage their time and talked with them about their opinions with time management. Finally, according to the results of the observation and talks, I listed the goals that my application need to achieve.

Step 1: User Interviews（Needfinding）

I asked several persons to talk about their current time management experience first and then let them show me their current methods of time management or time recording. After the interviews, I reviewed the whole talks and found their needs.

Here is my friend, called Xi Liu. She graduated last year and has little working experience. Sometimes, she cannot finish her work assigned by her boss in time and she has to work on weekend. So now time management is a big problem for her. She currently writes a daily report about what she has done today and what she will do tomorrow. Then she sent to her boss before she gets off work, However, there are some problems existed. For example, she always writes some works repeatedly, which is a little waste of time. And every time, she writes her daily report in a system and it cannot automatically calculate how much time a project last for.

Perhaps, we can record some repeatedd works as a model and we can easy to reuse it or we can make some works as a daily or weekly repeated task. In addition, except the start time and end time of a project are recorded, and the duration is also calculate in the system. It will be an important reference for next similar work.
